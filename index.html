Scaled Healthcare Claims Platform 400%

The Challenge

Automated claims processing platform needed to scale from 350M to 1.5B+ transactions annually while maintaining HIPAA/HITRUST compliance and growing revenue from $150M to $650M. Legacy waterfall delivery created bottlenecks and unpredictability.

My Approach

  • Transformed delivery across 14 programs from waterfall to SAFe, Scrum, and Kanban frameworks
  • Established quarterly PI Planning and portfolio governance in partnership with C-suite leaders
  • Introduced DevSecOps, CI/CD pipelines, and automated testing increasing deployment frequency 200%
  • Built Centers of Excellence for governance, tooling, and continuous improvement across 1,000+ IT staff

The Outcomes

  • Scaled platform from 350M to 1.5B+ transactions annually (428% growth)
  • Improved on-time delivery from 70% to 90% (2-3x better than industry baseline)
  • Grew platform revenue from $150M to $650M ARR
  • Reduced late-stage defects 70% and technical debt 60%
  • Increased team productivity 20%, contributed to Net Promoter Score of 73
